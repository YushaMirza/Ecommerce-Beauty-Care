Our Story

At Simplon Beauty, we believe beauty should be pure, compassionate, and accessible to everyone— regardless of age, race, gender, or background. Since 1985, we've been dedicated to offering high-quality, vegan, and cruelty-free products that reflect our unwavering commitment to ethical beauty. To us, beauty is about more than just appearance; it's about kindness, confidence, and celebrating what makes each person unique. What started as a single beauty care center has grown into a trusted name in cosmetics and accessories, with our stores now serving communities across multiple cities. Our reputation has been built on a foundation of exceptional service, timeless quality, and a genuine passion for making everyone feel welcomed and valued. At Simplon Beauty, we're more than just a brand—we're a community that embraces diversity and empowers people to express themselves freely. Whether you're discovering everyday essentials or indulging in luxurious self-care, we're here to make beauty a joyful, inclusive experience for everyone. Because true beauty is compassionate, conscious, and made for everyone.
